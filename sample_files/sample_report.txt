Q1 Business Performance Summary
--------------------------------

This report summarizes the key outcomes from the first quarter of the year.
We focus on revenue performance, customer adoption, operational efficiency,
and the actions taken to improve service quality.

Highlights
- Revenue grew year over year driven by new customer onboarding.
- Retention improved after the rollout of updated onboarding materials.
- Operational costs remained stable despite expanded delivery commitments.

Key Metrics
1. Total revenue: steady upward trend across the quarter.
2. Active accounts: increased consistently month over month.
3. Support tickets: decreased following knowledge base improvements.

Next Steps
- Expand pilot programs to additional regions.
- Continue investing in onboarding automation and documentation.
- Monitor churn drivers and refine segmentation for targeted outreach.
